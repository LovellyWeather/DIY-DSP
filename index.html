D I Y D S P

Learn Digital-Signal-Processing with the Pedal-Pi from Electrosmash

Welcome. Let's get started.

If this is your first time here.. Hello! This site is designed to guide brave souls through the land of Digital-Signal-Processing by building and programming a Digital-Effects Pedal! Throughout this guide you will learn DSP Terms and Concepts, circuit-board basics, some low-level programming with C, and a variety of digital effect types. To get started, head down to the Necessary Materials section. If you're returning, head to the section you're currently working through and get to it! Good luck!
